# pastyear

**Aliases:** pyr

## Description

Years for which past investments are specified; pastyears should usually be before the beginning of the first period but past investments may also be specified on later years.

## Related Indexes

- [modlyear](./modlyear.md)
- [year](./year.md)

## Usage

This index is used as a dimension in various TIMES parameters, sets, and equations. See the [Reference Manual Introduction](../../raw/docs/part-2/01-introduction.md) for the full notation conventions.
