# rpc_capflo (r,v,p,c)

**Category:** Internal
**Aliases:** None

## Description

Indicator that a commodity flow c in region r is associated with the capacity of a process (p, due to NCAP_ICOM, NCAP_OCOM, or NCAP_COM being provided).
