# PAR_COMBALEM

**Category:** Reporting
**Indexes:** r, t, c, s

## Description

Commodity Slack/Levels – Marginals: Undiscounted annual shadow price of commodity balance (EQE_COMBAL) being a strict equality. The marginal value describes the cost increase in the objective function, if the difference between production and consumption is increased by one unit. The marginal value can be determined by the production side (increasing production), but can also be set by the demand side (e.g., decrease of consumption by energy saving or substitution measures).
