# CST_COME

**Category:** Reporting
**Indexes:** r, t, c

## Description

Annual elastic demand cost term: Annual costs (losses) due to elastic demand changes of commodity (c). When elastic demands are used the objective function describes the total surplus of producers and consumers, which reaches its maximum in the equilibrium of demand and supply.
