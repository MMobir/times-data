# PAR_IPRIC

**Category:** Reporting
**Indexes:** r, t, p, c, s, uc_n

## Description

Inter-regional trade equations – Marginals: Undiscounted shadow price of the inter-regional trade equation of commodity (c) via exchange process (p) in period (t) and timeslice (s). The undiscounted shadow price can be interpreted as the import/export price of the traded commodity. Note: ucn={IMP/EXP}.
