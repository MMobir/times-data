# CST_IREC

**Category:** Reporting
**Indexes:** r, v, t, p, c

## Description

Annual implied costs of endogenous trade: Annual undiscounted costs from endogenous imports/exports of commodity (c) in period (t) associated with process (p) and vintage period (v), valued according to the marginal(s) of the trade equation of process p.
